Group Executive Verdict

Immediate Hardening Required

  • DNSSEC: Critical group-wide lack of DNSSEC across all domains, especially on ornate.in and AWS Route53 assets.
  • Email Auth: Zero visible DMARC policies across all 4 domains. CEO fraud and brand spoofing risk is exceptionally high.
  • Registrar Safety: Exposed PII in WHOIS makes vishing attacks trivial. Mask records immediately.
  • SSL Coverage: skycitymall.in has only 50% SSL coverage (3/6 endpoints). Critical production endpoints on port 80.
  • Retail Scam Risk: oberoimall.com and skycitymall.in face critical brand abuse risk for fake coupon/discount scams.

Group Risk Score

8.2/10

Risk: Medium-High. Four high-value corporate domains with systemic exposure points. Retail domains face critical brand abuse risks.
